What are the symptoms of diabetes?
2:14 PM
Diabetes symptoms include increased thirst and urination, fatigue, blurred vision, and slow-healing wounds.

Type 1 symptoms appear quickly. Type 2 can develop gradually and go unnoticed.

If you're experiencing these, a fasting blood glucose test can confirm a diagnosis. See your doctor for proper evaluation.

When should I go to the ER?
2:15 PM
Go to the ER immediately for: chest pain, difficulty breathing, signs of stroke (face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty), severe allergic reactions, or blood sugar below 70 mg/dL with confusion.
